About the Event

Party in Pink is a community-driven fitness event where we dance to make a difference. Every step raises awareness and brings us closer to early detection and better outcomes.

Zumba + Wellness: Fun, easy-to-follow moves designed for all fitness levels.
Awareness: Quick insights on early detection and support resources.
Community: Inclusive and supportive — bring friends and family.
