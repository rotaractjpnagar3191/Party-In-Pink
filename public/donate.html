Who We Are

Party In Pink is a passion project of Rotaract Club of Bangalore JP Nagar, a service organization dedicated to making a difference in our community. Our mission is to combine fun, fitness, and fundraising to support critical healthcare initiatives.

Our Impact

Since 2021, we've been organizing Party In Pink events, combining the power of Zumba with fundraising for breast cancer awareness and support. Every rupee raised directly supports Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital in their mission to provide quality cancer care.
