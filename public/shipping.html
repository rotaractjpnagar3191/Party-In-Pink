Party In Pink tickets and passes are delivered exclusively via email. There are no physical tickets, no postal delivery, and no on-site ticketing. Your tickets are digital and must be presented at the event via QR code.

How Tickets Are Delivered

  1. Payment Confirmation: After your payment is processed successfully, you will receive an immediate confirmation email.
  2. Ticket Issuance: Your tickets are issued through KonfHub, our digital ticketing partner, within 30 minutes of payment.
  3. Email Delivery: A ticket email will be sent to the email address provided during registration. This email contains:
    • Your ticket(s) with unique QR code(s)
    • Event details and venue information
    • Check-in instructions
    • Any important updates or changes

What to Do With Your Tickets

  • Download and Save: Download the ticket from the email and save it on your phone or print a hard copy.
  • QR Code at Event: Show the QR code at the event check-in to be scanned.
  • Digital Presentation Preferred: We recommend showing the QR code on your mobile device to avoid printing issues.

Didn't Receive Your Ticket?

If you haven't received your ticket email within 30 minutes of payment:

  • Check your spam/promotions folder
  • Verify the email address you provided is correct
  • Check your registration confirmation via our order status page
  • Contact us immediately with your order ID or email

Lost or Forgotten Your Ticket?

Tickets are tied to your registered email address. You can retrieve your tickets at any time by:

  1. Visiting the order status page
  2. Searching for your order using your email address or registration ID
  3. Requesting tickets be resent to your email

For Bulk Registrations

Important for club/organization coordinators:

  • Tickets are issued to your registered email address, not to individual participants
  • You will receive all passes in a single email with multiple QR codes (one per pass)
  • It is your responsibility to distribute the tickets to your group members
  • Keep the original email safeâ€”it contains all your group's QR codes
  • You can forward the email or individual QR codes to your group members

Privacy & Security

Your ticket emails are sent through secure channels and contain information specific to your registration. Do not share your QR codes publicly as they identify your registration. For more information, see our Privacy Policy.

Technical Issues?

If you encounter any technical issues accessing or displaying your tickets, please contact us for assistance.
