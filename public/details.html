Important Information

Who We Are

Rotaract Club of Bangalore JP Nagar is a service-oriented organization dedicated to community development, social responsibility, and humanitarian causes. Party In Pink is our flagship initiative for breast cancer awareness and fundraising.

Our Mission

To raise awareness about breast cancer, support affected individuals and families, and channel funds toward research and treatment initiatives through community engagement and fundraising events.

Fund Utilization

100% of donations and a significant portion of registration fees go toward our breast cancer awareness mission, including medical aid, awareness campaigns, and community support programs.

Event Safety

Party In Pink is conducted in adherence to all local health and safety guidelines. We maintain protocols for participant safety, hygiene, and crowd management.
