ALL REGISTRATIONS AND DONATIONS ARE FINAL AND NON-REFUNDABLE. Once you have completed your registration or made a donation to Party In Pink, no refunds will be issued under any circumstances.

Registration Policy

All event registrations are non-refundable. By registering for Party In Pink, you acknowledge and accept this policy. Please ensure you can attend the event before completing your registration.

Note: You may request to transfer your registration to another person, subject to availability and our approval. Contact us at rotaractjpnagar@gmail.com for transfer requests.

Donation Policy

Donations made to Party In Pink are completely non-refundable. All donations are contributions to our breast cancer awareness cause and are final transactions. We appreciate your support, but please be certain of your donation amount before proceeding.

Payment Issues & Exceptions

If you encounter a technical error or duplicate charge, please contact us immediately at rotaractjpnagar@gmail.com with proof of payment. We will investigate and resolve technical issues on a case-by-case basis.

Event Postponement or Cancellation

In the rare event that Party In Pink is postponed or cancelled due to unforeseen circumstances (severe weather, natural disaster, etc.), we will offer options including:

  • Transfer of registration to the rescheduled event
  • Full refund (only in this scenario)

Any refund in this scenario will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

Contact Us

For any questions about our no-refund policy or to discuss exceptions, please reach out to us at rotaractjpnagar@gmail.com.
