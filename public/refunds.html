All Sales Are Final and Non-Refundable

Party In Pink operates a strict NO-REFUND and NO-CANCELLATION policy. All ticket and pass purchases are final and cannot be refunded, cancelled, or exchanged under any circumstances.

What This Means

  • Digital Tickets: Once a ticket is issued to your registered email address, it cannot be cancelled or refunded.
  • No Partial Refunds: If you purchased multiple passes, all of them are non-refundable.
  • No Event Cancellations: Even if the event is postponed, cancelled, or rescheduled, we cannot issue refunds. Existing tickets remain valid for the rescheduled date.
  • No Exceptions: This policy applies universally‚Äîno exceptions for any reason, including illness, schedule conflicts, or personal circumstances.

Donations

Donations are also non-refundable. Once a donation is processed, it is final and cannot be reversed. Donations support our charitable initiatives, and funds are allocated immediately after receipt.

What If There's an Issue?

If you encounter any technical issues or believe there's been an error with your registration, please contact us immediately. We will investigate and assist where possible, but refunds will not be issued.

Duplicate Transactions

If you accidentally made duplicate purchases or multiple transactions, please contact us as soon as possible. We have measures to prevent and address duplicate charges, but the onus is on you to report this within 24 hours of the transaction.

Payment Method Issues

If your bank or payment provider declined your transaction or reversed a charge, we will not reissue a ticket or accept another payment attempt. You will need to resolve the matter with your financial institution directly.

Why This Policy?

Party In Pink is a non-profit charity event organized by the Rotaract Club of Bangalore JP Nagar. Our strict no-refund policy ensures:

  • Funds are reliably allocated to our charitable cause.
  • We can accurately forecast event logistics and resource allocation.
  • Operational costs are covered without dependency on refund contingencies.
  • All collected funds support our community impact initiatives.
