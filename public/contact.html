Common Questions

Where is the event?

For event venue details, please visit our main event page or email us at rotaractjpnagar@gmail.com

When is Party In Pink happening?

Event date and time information is available on the event page. Check your confirmation email for all details.

I didn't receive my ticket email

Please check your spam/promotions folder first. Then visit our order status page to check your registration. If you need further help, email us with your registration details.

Can I get a refund?

No. Party In Pink operates a strict no-refund policy. Please see our Refunds & Cancellations page for complete details.

How do I check my order status?

Visit our order status page and search by your email address or order ID to see your registration details and ticket status.

I have a payment issue

Payment issues should be directed to your bank or payment provider first. For registration-related payment questions, please email us at rotaractjpnagar@gmail.com with your email address and order details.

What about my personal data?

For privacy and data security inquiries, please review our Privacy Policy or email us. We take data protection seriously and comply with applicable privacy regulations.

About Party In Pink

Party In Pink is a Zumba fundraiser organized by the Rotaract Club of Bangalore JP Nagar, a service organization committed to community development and charitable causes. All proceeds from this event support our community impact initiatives.
