Understanding our mission and community

What is Rotary?

Rotary is a worldwide organization of business and professional leaders united by the ideal of service. It brings together people from all walks of life who are committed to advancing world understanding, goodwill, and peace through their personal, professional, and community endeavors.

Rotary's Motto: "Service Above Self"

This guiding principle inspires Rotarians to take action to serve others while strengthening their own communities.

What is Rotaract?

Rotaract is an international service organization for young professionals aged 18-40. Founded in 1968, it offers young adults the opportunity to exchange ideas and take action with like-minded peers. Rotaractors tackle issues like poverty, hunger, disease, illiteracy, environmental degradation, and gender inequality through community service projects.
