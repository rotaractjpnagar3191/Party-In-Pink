Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Party In Pink ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by the Rotaract Club of Bangalore JP Nagar. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and handle your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Registration Information: Name, email address, phone number, organization name (for bulk registrations), and event preferences.
  • Payment Information: We do not directly store payment card details. Payment processing is handled by our third-party payment gateway provider, Cashfree Payments or KonfHub.
  • Event Data: Attendance records, ticket passes, and scanning data for event check-in purposes.
  • Communication Data: Your email address and phone number for sending registration confirmations, ticket details, and event updates.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Processing your registration and payment for Party In Pink events.
  • Issuing your digital tickets and passes via KonfHub.
  • Sending you event confirmations, updates, and important notices.
  • Identifying you at the event during check-in via QR code scanning.
  • Improving our event experience and services.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your information:

  • Cashfree Payments: Payment processing. Their privacy practices are available at https://cashfree.com/privacy-policy
  • KonfHub: Ticket issuance and event management. Their privacy practices are available at https://konfhub.com/privacy
  • Netlify: Website hosting and function execution. Their privacy practices are available at https://www.netlify.com/privacy

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Payment information is encrypted and processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, typically for the duration of the event and for 1 year afterward for accounting and legal compliance purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications (though event-critical notifications may still be sent).

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

8. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses session cookies to store your form data temporarily (for timeout recovery) and to enhance your experience. We do not use cookies for third-party advertising or tracking.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us.

This Privacy Policy is provided for informational purposes and is subject to the Terms and Conditions of Party In Pink.
